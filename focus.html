Image Processing & Computer Vision

Image Processing and Computer Vision are the most exciting fields in Machine Learning and AI. Image processing is a way to convert an image to a digital aspect and perform certain functions on it to get an enhanced image or extract other useful information from it. It is a type of signal time when the input is an image, such as a video frame or image, and the output can be an image or features associated with that image. We will cover the following techniques under the image process are:

  • Image Acquisition
  • Image Enhancement
  • Image Restoration
  • Color Image Processing
  • Wavelets and Multiresolution Processing

Bioinformatics

Bioinformatics is a field of computational science that has to do with analyzing sequences of biological molecules. It usually refers to genes, DNA, RNA, or protein, and is particularly useful in comparing genes and other sequences in proteins and other sequences within an organism or between organisms, looking at evolutionary relationships between organisms, and using the patterns that exist across DNA and protein sequences to figure out what their function is. We can think about bioinformatics as essentially the linguistics part of genetics. That is, linguistics people look at patterns in language, and that's what bioinformatics people do--looking for patterns within sequences of DNA or protein. The techniques covered in the bioinformatics area:

  • Next-generation sequencing data analysis
  • Multi-omics data integration and analysis
  • Personalized medicine and disease modeling
  • Chemoinformatic (modeling and prediction)

Computational Biology

Computational biology uses data analysis, mathematical modeling, and computational simulations to understand biological systems and relationships. An intersection of computer science, biology, and big data, the field also has applied mathematics, chemistry, and genetics foundations. It differs from biological computing, a subfield of computer engineering which uses bioengineering to build computers.

  • Data and modeling
  • Systems Biology
  • Evolutionary biology
  • Neuroscience
